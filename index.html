How it works

cc: David@lyte.ai in your email Tag david in the body of the email with @david [your request]

Status updates
@david Get an update from my team every Monday morning at 9am
David requests an update from everyone in the email thread at the requested time, while keeping you in the loop

Reminders
@david Remind Guneet to pick up her access badge by 5pm
David reminds Guneet by emailing her in the same email thread at the scheduled time

Follow-up
@david Follow-up with Paul on termsheet review next Friday
If no response is received by the requested date then David follows-up with Paul in the same email thread. If a response is received from Paul then David ignores the follow-up request

Tasks
@david Web design task is due next week by Lilia
David reminds Lilia before the due date, asks for an update on the due date and follows-up after the due date if the task is not complete, while keeping you in the loop
@david stop to stop an action
@david change [your new request] to modify an action
